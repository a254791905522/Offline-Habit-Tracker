  • Track current and longest streaks per habit

  • Visual progress ring for daily completion
  • Celebrate when you finish every habit for the day

Beautiful statistics

  • Interactive calendar heatmap for each habit

  • Success rate and total completed days

Daily reminders

  • Set custom reminder times per habit
  • Gentle notifications — enable or disable per habit
