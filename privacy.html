Privacy

Effective April 2026

The app is built so your habit data never needs to leave your device. We do not operate accounts, servers, or analytics for this product.

What this app does with data

Offline — Habit Tracker stores your habits, check-ins, streaks, reminders, and exported files on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac. That information is processed and retained locally by iOS, iPadOS, or macOS unless you choose to share it yourself (for example by sending an export).

As stated on the App Store, the developer does not collect data from this app for tracking or marketing. There are no third-party analytics SDKs, ad networks, or cloud sync tied to the app in its default operation.

Notifications

If you turn on reminders, the system schedules local notifications on your device. You can disable them anytime in the app or in system Settings. No reminder content is sent to us.

Exports

When you export data as JSON, that file is created on your device. What you do with the file (save, email, back up) is under your control and subject to those services’ own policies.

Children

The app is rated 9+ on the App Store. It does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. There is no social feed or chat; use is limited to local habit tracking.

Changes

If our practices change in a future version, we will update this page and, where appropriate, note changes in the app or on the store listing.
